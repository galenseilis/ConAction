Glossary of Terms
=================

.. glossary::

    Coefficient of determination
        The square of the Pearson's product-moment correlation coefficient.

    Correlation
        Either a linear relationship between linear variables or a statistical dependence in general.

    Kendall rank correlation coefficient
        The average of the number of concordant pairs minus the number of discordant pairs.

    Pearson product-momment correlation coefficient
        A bilinear function that quantifies the linear relationship between two random variables.

    Spearman's rho correlation coefficient
        The Pearson's product-moment correlation coefficient computed on the ranks of random variables.

    Trinity of Covariation
        A metaphor stating that covariation is coordinated changes in structures.
